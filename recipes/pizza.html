Pizza Sauce

Description

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by
well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, coca cola.

Make the dough Before following these steps !

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces low moisture mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 leaves fresh basil, torn

Steps

  1. Lay 1/2 of the mozzarella on the crust, then sprinkle 1/4 teaspoon oregano and 1/8 teaspoon pepper over the top
    Randomly arrange 1/4 cup crushed tomatoes over the pizza,
    leaving some empty areas, then drizzle 1 tablespoon olive oil over top.
  2. With a quick back and forth jerk,
    make sure dough will release from the peel easily
    Place the tip of the peel at the back of the preheated pizza stone,
    and remove the peel so that the pizza is left on the stone.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust begins to brown, 4 to 6 minutes.
  4. Slide the pizza onto the peel and remove from the oven;
    then slide off the peel onto a work surface. Sprinkle 1/2 of the basil leaves randomly over the pizza
    Cut into wedges and serve.
